The Four Historic Houses

Hogwarts is divided into four legendary houses, each founded by one of the great historical sorcerers of their age. These houses act as your community, your family, and your academic anchor throughout your time at the college. Your house assignment reflects your core values, potential, and underlying character.

Gryffindor
Bravery • Daring • Chivalry

Founded by Godric Gryffindor, this house values nerve, courage, and standing up for what is right, carrying the symbol of the noble lion.

Slytherin
Ambition • Cunning • Leadership

Founded by Salazar Slytherin, its members are resourceful, highly ambitious, and strategic leaders driven by a collective hunger to achieve greatness.

Ravenclaw
Intelligence • Wisdom • Wit

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house prizes academic excellence, profound creativity, sharp curiosity, and individuality of thought.

Hufflepuff
Loyalty • Patience • Hard Work

Founded by Helga Hufflepuff, this house values fair play, absolute loyalty, relentless work ethic, and unconditional kindness toward everyone.
